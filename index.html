Inimesed

An Android app that lets you search your contacts by voice. Internet not required. Based on Pocketsphinx. Uses Estonian acoustic models.

Inimesed is a simple app that lets you search and pick a contact from your address book. The focus of Inimesed is solely on search, making the contact picking action as fast and hands-free as possible. To that end the app employs search-by-voice.

Used resources

The built-in speech recognizer is based on Pocketsphinx by the CMU Sphinx project, specifically its Android port, see Building Pocketsphinx On Android.

The Estonian acoustic models have been created by the Laboratory of Phonetics and Speech Technology, Institute of Cybernetics at Tallinn University of Technology and are available at github.com/alumae/et-pocketsphinx-tutorial.
